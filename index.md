## 🧩 Technical Concepts

v2rayF functions as a proxy client. This means it creates a tunnel between your computer and a server elsewhere. Your web browser sends requests to this tunnel instead of sending them directly to the internet. The server receives your request, fetches the webpage, and sends the back through the tunnel. This process keeps your IP address hidden and hides the content of your requests from your network provider. This architecture is the core of modern secure browsing. By using the Avalonia framework, the app remains responsive and light on your system resources. The use of proxy protocols like VLESS ensures that even if someone inspects your traffic, they see encrypted noise rather than your actual activity.
